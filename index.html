Biography

I am a third-year Computer Science major with a long, accomplished history in both the technological and educational worlds. As a strong public speaker, educator, and researcher, I have for years worked to educate and inspire people on the wondrous feats of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our modern society simply wouldn't be possible without the massive propagation of technology throughout it. I am motivated by fueling that propagation through education and research. I have always had a passion for the sciences, at just ten years old, I constructed a fully articulated robotic arm to show off at my elementary school's science fair. I am currently an Instructional Design Intern at the University of Maryland, College Park. This position allows me to apply my skills in technology and education with such responsibilities as creating course content, designing webpages, conducting research, and much more. I aspire to become a full researcher in STEM. When I'm not juggling these, I enjoy rollerskating, driving, puzzles, adventuring with friends, performing in local shows, and building robots.
